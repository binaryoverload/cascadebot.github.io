Features

Customisation

Cascade has been specifically designed with ultimate customisation in mind. Whether it's using our incredibly detailed permission system or disabling or renaming individual commands. You name it; you can change it!

Moderation

Cascade's developers worked hard to make intuitive and useful moderation commands. No need to stress about rule-breakers, because Cascade makes it effortless to put them in their place!

Music

Cascade uses a distributed audio system to allow for seamless streaming of your favourite music. Sit back, relax, and listen to decent beats with your squad. There's no reason not to love it!
